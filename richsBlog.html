HOW TO WRITE A JOB WINING RESUME.

CONTACT DETAILS

Write your name, phone number, official email address and you can add your LinkedIn profile if you have. Ensure the email address you are using has your official names and not nick name. Adding a photo to your profile next to your contact details brings out your personality but it is not a necessity unless you have been asked to. It is optional.

PERSONAL PROFILE

This is also referred to as summary. This is a short paragraph summarizing your career experience, qualification and skills. Describe who you are using a few words. Look at this example:-

EDUCATION BACKGROUND/EDUCATION

List your educational qualification in reverse chronological order. Start with the most recent qualification acquired. Write your academic year/year attended, name of institution and qualification attained. If you acquired a PhD or Masters level leave out institutions attended more than ten years ago e.g. primary school attended.

SKILLS SECTION

List all soft and hard skills that you have. Ensure you read the job description and competencies required before you fill out this section. Ensure you give supporting evidence that you have the skills that you have listed.

TIP: There are soft skills that are considered redundant. Do not include them

EXPERIENCE

Start with the most recently acquired experience. State the organization/employer and position held. As you describe your experience, focus on achievement. What did you do to save time, make the process more efficient and had a lasting impact on the organization or company or fellow employees. Ensure you put a minimum of three job experiences acquired.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

Describe any other important information that you would like the recruiter to know. For example:- Awards. Trainings attended. Leadership. Hobbies.

REFEREES

Only include this section if the job advert instructs you to do so. And if you do include referees, ensure you have notified them of the same. Referees should be people who know you very well and can testify on your behalf. Be careful when selecting referees.

NOTE

Many companies/ recruiters receive hundreds if not thousands of job applications. The recruiters therefore use an Applicant Tracking System software to rank resumes. Your resume may never be read as this ATS system filters resumes by picking up only necessary information and keywords in your resume. To learn more about this, check it out in my next video.
